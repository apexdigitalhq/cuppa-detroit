Our Story

Cuppa Detroit started as a homegrown dream — built from my own kitchen with a passion for coffee, creativity, and community. Every detail has been created by me, from designing the coffee cart to developing our drinks and advertising. What began as late-night learning and experimenting has grown into a mobile espresso bar, serving pop-ups, private events, and gatherings across Metro Detroit.

For me, coffee is never just a transaction. It's about real conversations, meaningful connections, and creating moments that feel warm and personal.

My goal is to continue growing Cuppa Detroit and one day open a brick-and-mortar café where the community can come together and feel at home. Until then, you'll find us around the city — brewing with intention and serving Detroit one cup at a time.
