Our Story

From Our Kitchen
to Your City 💛

It all started at home — just a girl, a dream, and way too many latte experiments in her kitchen. What began as a passion project quickly became something bigger.

Now we're Detroit's mobile coffee cart, popping up at markets, events, and celebrations across the city. Every cup we serve carries that same homemade love from day one.

The dream? Our own brick & mortar coffee shop someday. A cozy little spot where the community can gather, sip something beautiful, and feel at home. We're not there yet — but we're brewing our way closer every single day. 🤎
