Our Story

Cuppa Detroit is a mobile espresso bar bringing handcrafted specialty drinks to weddings, private events, pop-ups, corporate functions, and community experiences across Metro Detroit.

We've already served multiple events and we're just getting started. Every cup is crafted with care — from our signature lattes to our famous hot cocoa. We bring the full coffee shop experience directly to your event.
