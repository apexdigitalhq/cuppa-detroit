About Us

Cuppa Detroit is a luxury mobile espresso bar serving handcrafted specialty drinks for weddings, private events, pop-ups, corporate functions, brand collaborations, and community experiences across Metro Detroit. We offer both large event packages and packages designed for smaller gatherings.
